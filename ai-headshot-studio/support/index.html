Frequently Asked

Why do my headshots say "Preview mode"?

The current version runs entirely on your device: your headshots are styled framings of your own selfies, clearly labeled as previews. Full AI generation is coming in a future update, and the app will tell you exactly when your photos are sent to our generation server.

Is the app really free?

Yes — free and unlimited while in beta. No account, no ads, no subscription.

Where are my photos stored?

In preview mode, everything stays on your device. Selfies and generated sets live in the app's local storage; deleting a set in History removes it permanently. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

How do I save or share a headshot?

On any result, tap Save to add it to your photo library (the app will ask for permission the first time) or Share to send it by AirDrop, Messages, Mail, or any other app.
