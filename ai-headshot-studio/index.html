Privacy Policy — AI Headshot Studio

Effective date: 2026-07-20

App: AI Headshot Studio (iOS)

Publisher: Bull Creek Business Solutions

Contact: caleb@bullcreekbusinesssolutions.io

Summary

AI Headshot Studio turns your selfies into styled profile photos. In the current version (preview mode) all photo processing happens entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Information We Collect

None. AI Headshot Studio does not require an account, does not ask for your name, email, contacts, or location, and does not include analytics or advertising SDKs. In preview mode the app makes no network connections at all.

Camera, Photos, and Your Selfies

The app uses your device's camera and photo library solely so you can provide the selfies your headshots are made from. In preview mode, those selfies and every generated headshot are processed and stored locally on your device and never leave it unless you choose to save a headshot to your photo library or share it through the iOS share sheet. Deleting a set in History permanently removes those images from the app's storage on your device.

When AI Generation Ships

A future update will add true AI generation. When you use that mode, the selfies you select will be sent over an encrypted connection to our generation server, used only to create your headshots, and deleted from the server immediately after processing. They will not be used to train AI models, will not be linked to your identity, and will never be sold or shared. The app will clearly indicate when AI mode is active, and this policy will be updated (with a new effective date) before that feature launches.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any personal data, because we do not collect any. Images you save or share go only to the destination you pick.

Children's Privacy

AI Headshot Studio is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content. It is not directed at children and, because it collects no data, we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date above and post the revised policy at the same URL.
