Privacy Policy — Merchant Fee Calculator

Effective date: 2026-07-17

App: Merchant Fee Calculator (iOS)

Publisher: Bull Creek Business Solutions

Contact: caleb@bullcreekbusinesssolutions.io

Summary

Merchant Fee Calculator helps business owners understand what they pay to accept credit cards. We designed it to work entirely offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Information We Collect

None. Merchant Fee Calculator does not require an account, does not ask for your name, email, contacts, photos, or location, and does not include analytics or advertising SDKs. The app makes no network connections at all. The statement numbers you enter and any reviews you save are stored locally on your device and never leave it.

How Your Numbers Are Used

Everything you enter — card volume, fees, transaction counts, and rates — is used only on your device to calculate your effective rate and overpayment estimate. Saved reviews live only on your phone. Nothing is uploaded, anywhere.

Sharing and Contacting Us

If you choose to share a review summary through the iOS share sheet, or to email us for a free statement review, that action is initiated entirely by you. Emailing us opens a draft in your own mail app; we only receive what you decide to send.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any personal data, because we do not collect any.

Children's Privacy

Merchant Fee Calculator is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content. It is not directed at children and, because it collects no data, we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date above and post the revised policy at the same URL.
