Frequently Asked

Do I need an account to book?

No. Bookdout books you in as a guest — pick a service, pick an open time, enter your name, email, and phone, and confirm. Your confirmation number appears immediately and the app remembers your bookings on your device under My Bookings.

How do I cancel an appointment?

Open My Bookings, choose the appointment, and tap Cancel. Cancellations more than 48 hours before the appointment are free. Within 24–48 hours, the app will ask you to contact the provider directly. Inside 24 hours a late-cancellation fee may apply per the provider's policy (shown in the app).

How do I get the appointment into my calendar?

On the confirmation screen (or in My Bookings), tap "Add to Calendar" — the app opens a standard calendar file that your device's calendar app can save.

How do I pay?

The app never asks for card details. You pay the provider directly — for example, in person at your appointment.

I booked on another device — why isn't it in My Bookings?

Guest bookings are remembered on the device you booked from. If you need help with a booking made elsewhere, contact the provider or email us.
