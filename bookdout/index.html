Privacy Policy — Bookdout

Effective date: 2026-07-20

App: Bookdout (iOS)

Publisher: Bull Creek Business Solutions

Contact: caleb@bullcreekbusinesssolutions.io

Summary

Bookdout lets you book appointments with a service provider as a guest — no account required. The only personal information the app handles is the contact information you type in to make a booking: your name, email address, and phone number. That information is sent over an encrypted connection to our booking service (bookdout.io) and shared with the provider you are booking with, so they know who the appointment is for and can contact you about it (for example, appointment confirmations and reminders). Nothing else is collected: no tracking, no advertising, no analytics SDKs, no location, no contacts, no account profile.

Information We Collect

Booking details you provide: your name, email address, phone number, and the service, date, and time you choose. This is used solely to create, confirm, manage, and (if you choose) cancel your appointment, and to let the provider contact you about it. We do not use it for marketing, we do not sell it, and we do not share it with anyone other than the provider you booked with and the infrastructure that hosts our booking service.

Stored on your device: for convenience, the app remembers your contact details and your bookings locally on your device so you can rebook faster and manage your appointments. This local data never leaves your device except as part of a booking you submit, and you can remove it by deleting the app.

What We Do Not Collect

No account is required and none is created for you. The app contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs, and does not access your location, contacts, photos, or any other device data. We do not track you across apps or websites.

Payments

The app does not collect card details. Any payment for services happens directly with the provider (for example, in person at the time of your appointment). A late-cancellation fee may apply per the provider's posted cancellation policy, handled directly by the provider.

Calendar Files

If you tap "Add to Calendar", the app opens a standard calendar (.ics) file describing your appointment. Adding it to your calendar is handled by your device's calendar app; we receive nothing from your calendar.

Data Retention and Deletion

Booking records are kept by the booking service so your provider has an accurate appointment history. To ask for a booking record to be corrected or deleted, contact us at the address below or contact your provider directly, and we will handle it promptly.

Children's Privacy

Bookdout is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date above and post the revised policy at the same URL.
