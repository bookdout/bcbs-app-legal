Summary

AI Teleprompter is a teleprompter app for reading scripts on your device. We designed it to work entirely offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Information We Collect

None. AI Teleprompter does not require an account, does not ask for your name, email, contacts, photos, or location, and does not include analytics or advertising SDKs. The app makes no network connections at all. Your scripts and settings are stored locally on your device and never leave it.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any personal data, because we do not collect any.

Children's Privacy

AI Teleprompter is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content. It is not directed at children and, because it collects no data, we do not knowingly collect information from children.
